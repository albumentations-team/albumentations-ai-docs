# Chinese Blog Post Guidelines

## Platform-Specific

### Zhihu

- **Excerpt = first two sentences.** Zhihu uses the first two sentences as the article excerpt. Put the hook there — the core claim, the problem, or the payoff. Do not bury it in paragraph 3.
- Structure the opening so the excerpt alone makes readers want to click.

### Juejin / CSDN

- Hook and "先说结论" (conclusion-first) style work well.
- Keep code blocks, tool links, and checklists prominent.

## Credibility (All Platforms)

Add credibility to every blog post in a **non–self-bragging** way. Weave it in naturally, not as a boast.

**Relevant credentials:**
- Kaggle Grandmaster
- 10+ years in computer vision
- 7 of those years working on Albumentations

**How to add it:**
- One short line near the top or in a brief author note
- Phrase as context ("基于过去 10 年在计算机视觉方向的工作经验，包括 7 年参与 Albumentations 的开发") rather than a list of achievements
- Avoid superlatives ("顶尖"、"最牛") — let the content speak
- Link to Kaggle profile or Google Scholar only if it fits the tone; do not over-link

**Example (natural):**
> 本文基于过去 10 年在计算机视觉方向的实际工作经验写成，其中 7 年参与 Albumentations 的开发。

**Avoid:**
> 我是 Kaggle Grandmaster，拥有 10+ 年 CV 经验，Albumentations 核心开发者，本文将教你...
